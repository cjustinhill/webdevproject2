Bad Bunny

The Biggest Artist in the World

Benito Martinez, AKA Bad Bunny, was born in San Juan, Puerto Rico. He grew up in a nearby small town called Vega Baja. He was an overall pretty average kid; he stayed out of trouble, went to church with his grandmother, and liked to spend time at home with his family.

He discovered a passion for music when he was 13 and began making his own interpolations of already popular songs. He began publishing his music on the platform Soundcloud..

As a teenager, Benito worked at a grocery store, bagging groceries at the check out lanes, but still had aspirations to succeed in music.

Eventually one of his Soundcloud songs was discovered by DJ Luian, who signed Benito to his record label.

Fame

Bad Bunny immediately took over. He started making trap songs which were now popular in Puerto Rico because of Anuel. This helped him get a name in the industry and eventually help him define his own sound.

Almost out of nowhere, Bad Bunny began collaborating with some of the world's biggest artists. He released multiple singles with J. Balvin, and a single with American Rapper Drake.

By the time Bad Bunny released his first studio album, he had already accomplished what few artists could hope to do.

Dominance

Since his single with Drake in 2018, Bad Bunny has released 5 studio albums and a mixtape that have dominated the world. His songs from years past are still on the Billboard Hot100, and his most recent work fills up most of the list.

Bad Bunny performing in Yankee Stadium in 2022.

This past year, Bad Bunny became the first artist to win the title of most-listened artist 3 years in a row, and was also named Apple Music artist of the year.

It seems that no matter what Bad Bunny seems to touch goes multi-platinum, and although the artist has said he will use 2023 to "rest," fans still expect a lot in the years to come from such a young artist.
