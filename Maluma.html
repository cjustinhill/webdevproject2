Maluma

Hometown Hero and International Superstar

Maluma was born in Medellin, Colombia as Juan Luis Londo√±o. As a kid, Juan was a talented soccer player, andd was able to compete for the youth division teams for two of Medellins professional club teams.

However, Juan was also a talented singer and found more passion and enjoyment in music. When he turned 15, his uncle paid for studio time as a gift, so that Juan could record his own song.

His talent was quickly noted by studio executives and he was soon signed to a record deal, when asked what his stage name would be he chose "Maluma" combining the first two letters of his parents and sisters names.

By the age of 21, Maluma had released 3 studio albums and became famous in Colombia, and in the latin community in general.

Pride of Colombia

Although Maluma is known for having a very pop-like persona and style, he has done very well in representing his culture. He has kept his thick Colombian accent at the expense of criticism from the Puerto-Rican dominated market.

He has always incorporated parts of Colombia's culture into latin pop, reggaeton, and he has even done a salsa song with Marc Anthony. No matter where he has gone, he has not forgotten his roots.

Worldwide Sensation

As is the case for many latin artists, Maluma has seen an explosion of popularity during the last few years. He began ad campaigns for popular designer brands in Europe, voiced a character in the Pixar film Encanto, and acted in a movie alongside Jennifer Lopez and Owen Wilson.

Maluma performing in Medellin, Colombia.

Now, with colloborations with English-speaking artists such as the Weeknd, Adam Levine, and many others, Maluma is quickly becoming a household name around the world. One of his most recent accomplishments was the honor of performing the song of the 2022 World Cup.
