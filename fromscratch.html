Why learn about Reggaeton?

  1. Reggaeton is one of the worlds fastest growing genres
    • As Spotify grew from 10 million users in May 2014 to 140 million users in June 2017, the reggaeton genre’s share of all Spotify listening grew 119 percent.
    • By comparison, pop’s share increased 13 percent over the same period, and country music’s share 4 percent.

  2. Helps us understand another culture
    • The origins of reggaeton, as well as many of its current themes, derive from the diaspora of Afro-Caribbean/Latino peoples. Learning about reggaeton helps us learn about their experiences.
    • As reggaeton becomes international, it is becoming a more important part of other cultures and influencing genres worldwide.

  3. Reggaeton is awesome!
    • It's popular for a reason! Reggaeton is the perfect genre for making you dance.
    • Plus it's always good to learn about something unfamiliar, especially if it's becoming increasingly popular around the world.
